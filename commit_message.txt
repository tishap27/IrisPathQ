Add: QAOA quantum optimizer for route selection

Implement quantum approximate optimization algorithm (QAOA) to solve
flight route optimization problem. The optimizer:

- Loads cost matrix from C preprocessing (fuel costs + conflict penalties)
- Encodes route selection as QUBO problem
- Uses 10-qubit QAOA circuit (2 qubits per flight)
- Compares quantum solution against classical greedy 
- Decodes measurement results to extract optimal route combination

Circuit design:
- Hadamard gates for superposition initialization
- Variational parameters: γ=1.0, β=0.5 (constant for demonstration)
- RZ gates for cost Hamiltonian encoding
- RX gates for mixer Hamiltonian
- Qiskit Aer simulator with 500 shots

- Currently Quantum solution matches greedy solution as there are no conflicts and simple params introduced
- Each flight independently picks cheapest route
